Tell Your Story.
We Animate It.

Write a story in plain English — Parsia's AI translates it into our custom .story DSL, then a 7-phase compiler pipeline transforms it into a living, breathing 2.5D animated scene with characters, dialogue, emotions, and AI-generated backgrounds.

How It Works

From English to Animation in 3 Steps

1

Write in English

Type your story naturally — "Alice walked into a dark forest. A wolf appeared and growled."

2

AI → .story DSL

Our AI backend translates your text into Parsia's custom .story format with scenes, characters, and actions.

3

Compile & Animate

The 7-phase compiler lexes, parses, optimizes, and generates a live 2.5D animation you can watch and share.

Capabilities

Everything You Need to Bring Stories to Life

🔤

Custom DSL Compiler

A full 7-phase compiler pipeline — lexer, parser, semantic analysis, IR generation, optimization, and code generation — built from scratch.

✦

AI Story Translation

Write your narrative in plain English. Our AI-powered backend translates it into Parsia's .story DSL automatically.

🎨

AI-Generated Scenes

Every scene gets a unique, AI-generated background. Characters get procedural appearances based on role and context.

🎭

Character Animation

Characters enter, exit, move, speak with typewriter dialogue, and express emotions — all driven by compiled instructions.

⚡

IR Optimization

Constant folding, dead-store elimination, and peephole passes reduce instruction count while preserving correctness.

📦

Export & Share

Save animations as JSON, export to video, or share via a unique URL. Cloud persistence backed by Supabase.

The Language

Your English Becomes .story Code

You write: "A hero enters a dark forest, looks around nervously, then a villain appears." — Parsia's AI translates it into the DSL on the right, which the compiler then brings to life.

2 AI translates it to .story DSL

4 Out comes a live 2.5D animation
